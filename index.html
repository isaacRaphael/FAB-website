About Us

Fab Educational Books is a publishing and printing house for particularly standardized learning and teaching resources. Ranging from

  • poetry anthologies, fiction, non-fiction,
  • journals, science texts, history text.
  • inspirational books and professional development books

From award winning fiction to nationwide approved educational texts, our publications span across Early Childhood, Secondary and Tertiary Education. We cover practically everything - professional advice, editing, cover design, layout, e-book conversion, and lots more. With over 30 years of experience, proven credibility, top-notch creativity and quality, we are merely a phone call away to handling your book extremely personally and professionally.
